Meal Card Management (AR 600-38)

Home

What does BAS stand for?

Basic Allowance for Subsistance - typically referred to as seperate rations

If meals in the field are not eaten, is the Soldier still charged for the meal?

Yes

When are claims for missed meals authorized?

They are only authorized for meals missed due to mission requirements supported by individual headcount records

Who will issue, control, and review the meal card management system?

An individual who would normally perform personnel administration center (PAC) functions

When is a military member receiving BAS NOT required to pay for government meals?

When a patient is in the hospital

What is the purpose of the DA Form 4809?

It is a record of the disposition of all DD Forms 714 not attached to the Meal Card Control Book
