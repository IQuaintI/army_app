What does ADP stand for?

Army Doctrine Publication

Explain the ADP

Doctrine generally limited to approximately 10 pages and explains the fundamentals of the subject as well as how they support operations

What does ADRP stand for?

Army Doctrine Reference Publication

Explain the ADRP

Provides detailed explanation of all doctrinal principles which provide a foundational understanding so that everyone can interpret the doctrine in the same way. Usually limited to less than 100 pages.

What does ATP stand for?

Army Techniques Publications

Explain what ATPs contain

Consists of techniques that are non-prescriptive ways or methods used to perform

What is Army Doctrine?

Contains fundamental principles by which Army forces or elements thereof guide their actions in support of National objectives
