What is the primary regulation that covers the military policy on pay and allowances?

AR 37-104-4

What report does the commander review on a monthly basis concerning financial entitlement?

The Unit Commander's Finance Report (UCFR)

Name three hazardous duty incentives pays?

1. Parachute Duty
2. Flight deck duty
3. Demolition duty
4. Expiremental stress duty
5. Toxic fuels duty
6. Toxic pesticides duty
7. Dangerous virus's lab duty
8. Chemical munition

Regarding Family Care Plans, what financial actions must the service memebrs take>

They must complete the DD Form 2558 or proof of other adequate financial arrangements for care of family members

What does the acronym BAS stand for?

Basic Allowance for Subsistance

When would a Soldier recieve Rations in Kind not Available?

At permanent duty stations where government mess is not available or where it has been determined that it is impractical for the government to make meals available

What does the acronym RIKNA stand for?

Rations in Kind not Available

What does the acronym BAH stand for?

Basic Allowance Housing

What are the different types of BAH?

1. BAH-I
2. BAH-II
3. BAH(DIFF) which is the difference between with and without dependent rates of BAH-II
4. Partial BAH
5. Overseas Housing Allowance (OHA)
6.Family Seperation Housing (FSA)

When is an advance in pay authorized?

1. PCS
2. Evacuation of member or dependents
3. Advance of allotments to dependents
4. Advance for members of the Armed Forces Health Profession Scholarship Program
5. Advance of BAH
6. Advance of BAS only when an enlisted Soldier is ordered to a remote location where mess is not readily available
7. Advance pay for Senior Reserve Officers Training Corps

Name three times when justification is required for advanced pay?

1. A Soldier is requesting a second advance
2. An advance of more than 1 month basic pay without deductions
3. More than 12-month repayment schedule
4. Payment earlier than 30 days before departure
5.Payment later than 60 days after arrival at the new duty station

What will commanders do with regards to Soldiers who have local accounts of indebtedness who are about to leave their current duty station?

The commander will take every effort to ensure that the Soldier settles the accunt prior to departure, including UCMJ action if needed. This also applies to Soldiers that write checks against accounts with insufficient funds

Give two examples of informal unit funds a commander may authorize

1. Office Coffee
2. Cup and Flower
3. Annual Picnic Funds

When should the solicitation of informal unit funds be curtailed?

During the CFC and Army Emergency Relief campaign periods to reduce competition with this effort

What financial responsibility does a Soldier have prior to changing duty stations?

1. Make a reasonable effort to settle local accounts before movement
2. Take action to promptly settle organizational counts with local firms
