Define weapon control status.

Define tactical method of fire, control given by a leader that incorporates the tactical situation, rules of engagement for the area of operation and expected or anticipated enemy contact.

Define weapon control status "Weapons Hold".

Engage only if engaged or ordered to engage.

Define weapons control status "Weapons Tight".

Engage only if target is positively identified as enemy.

Define weapon control status "Weapons Free".

Engage targets not positively identified as friendly.

What is the definition of a mechanical zero?

The process of aligning the weapon sight system to a common start point.

What does WCS mean or stand for?

Weapon control status.
