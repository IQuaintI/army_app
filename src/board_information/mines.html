What happened on May 16, 1996 as it pertains to landmines?

The President of the United States implemented a phase restriction and elimination of anti-personnel landmines. The use of non-self-destructing anti-personnel landmines restricted to specific areas internationally recognized national borders and in established demilitarized zones such as South Korea.

When probing for mines, how far apart should you probe?

About every two inches.

When probing for mines, at what angle should the probe be used?

Less than 45 degrees.

What is the M131 modular pack mine system?

A man-carried portable anti-tank and anti-personnel mine system.

What is the M21 mine used for?

Anti-tank mission.

What should you do before probing for mines?

Leave your load bearing equipment and rifle with another member of the team. Keep your helmet and body armor on to protect you from possible blasts.

What device can be used to probe for mines.

A wooden stick at least 12 inches long.

What must you do to one of the end of your wooden probes?

Sharpen it.

How wide of a length should the soldier create as they clear the minefield?
