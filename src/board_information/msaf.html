What does MSAF stand for?

Multi-Source Assessment and Feedback or the 360 Assessment

Explain the MSAF in your own words

A tool that promotes sel-awareness for individual leader development. It provides feedback from your subordinates, peers, and superiors. The goal is to purge the ranks of toxic leaders

What does VIC stand for?

Virtual Improvement Center

What is the VIC?

An online resource of training and educational products that helps leaders and Soldiers learn and practice leadership

When conducting a self-assessment of the MSAF program, who will the individual recieve feedback from?

Superiors, peers, and subordinates

Who can request or take part in the MSAF program?

All individuals who possess a common access caed can register to participate in the MSAF program

When must all NCO's initiate and complete the Leader 360 or Unit 360?

At least every 36 months from the end of their last assessment

What is the purpose of the LDP?

To guide performance improvements

What does the acronym PDM stand for?

Professional Development Model

What are the goals of the MSAF program?

To create frank and open discussions, provide multiple viewpoints, personal insight, perceived leadership strengths and developmental needs, and recommended leader development resources

Describe the MSAF program as it relates to the Army's performance evaluation system

All MSAF programs are developmental in nature, separate, and distinct from the Army evaluation system

What are the three types of MSAF reports?

Individual, unit, and unspecified aggregate reports

Where will the results of the MSAF asessment be recorded?

It will be recorded on the evaluation report
