Foot Marches (ATP 3-21.28)

Home

Define a successful foot march?

When a Soldier arrive at their destination at the prescribed time and are physically and mentally capable of executing the mission

Before conducting a foot march, what are some things that can be done to preserve the feet?

1. Apply moleskin
2. Keep feet clean and dry
3. Use foot powder and change socks as needed

What is an administrative movement?

A movement in which troops and vehicles are arranged to expedite their movement and conserve time and energy when no enemy is expected

When is a tactical road march used?

When contact with the movement is likely

What dictates how a tactical road march will be conducted?

In accordance with METT-TC:
1. Mission
2. Enemy
3. Terrain amd weather
4. Troops and support available
5. Time available
6. Civil considerations
