Describe the javelin

The javelin is the first fire-and-forget, crew-served anti-tank missile. The javelin's two major components are a reusable Command Launch Unit (CLU) and a missile sealed in a disposable launch tube assembly.

What is the caliber of the missile the javelin fires?

126 mm

What is the maximum effective range of the javelin?

2,000 meters

What is the maximum effective engagement range of the javelin in direct attack mode?

65 meters

What is the maximum effective engagement range of the javelin in top attack mode?

150 meters
