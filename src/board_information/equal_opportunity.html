What does MEO stand for?

Military Equal Opportunity

What complaints does the MEO process address?

Complaints that allege unlawful discrimination on the basis of race, color, sex (to include gender identity), national origin, religion, or sexual orientation and harassment which includes hazing, bullying, and other discriminatory harassment

What are the protected categories of the Equal Opportunity program?

1. Race
2. Color
3. Gender
4. Religion
5. National origin

Explain the EO program?

Provides fair treatment for military personnel and family members without regards to race, color, gender, religion, national origin, sexual orientation, and provides an enviroment free of unlawful discrimination and offensive behavior

Define discrimination

An act that unlawfully results in unequal treatment of persons or groups based on race, color, gender, national origin, or religion

Define disparaging terms

1. Terms used to degrade or connote negative statements pertaining to race, color, gender, national origin, or religion
2. Name three of the seven methods in which disparaging terms can be expressed?
3. Verbal statements
4. Printed material
5. Visual material
6. Signs
7. Symbols
8. Posters
9. Insignia

Define gender identity

The internal perception, experience, or sense of being male or female. Includes how a person labels themselves based on how much they align or do not align with what they understand their opinion for gender to be. Common identity labels include man, woman, trans or transsexual, and gender fluid among others. Gender identity may or may not align with one's physical anatomy

Define sex discrimination

Occurs when an individual is deprived of an opportunity because of their sex (including gender identity and pregnancy) or when decisions are made based on stereotypes and assumptions about abilities, traits, or the performance of individuals on the basis of sex

How long does an individual have to make an EO complaint?

60 calender days from the date of the incident for a formal complaint

What is the difference between an informal and formal complaint?

A formal complaint is in writing

What are the three methods available to report or process an EO Complaint?

Anonymous, formal, and informal
