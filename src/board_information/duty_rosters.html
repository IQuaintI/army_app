Duty Roster (AR 220-45)

Home

Whose names are contained on DA FORM 6?

Only those Soldiers required to preform duty

What is the DA FORM 6?

The Duty Roster

How are names entered on the DA FORM 6?

Beginning alphabetically with the highest pay grade

When a new Soldier is placed on duty roster before the current roster is filled up where is their name placed?

At the bottom of the list

When a new Soldier is placed on duty roster after the current roster is filled up where is their name placed?

In the appropriate order alphabetically by pay grade

Normally, what Soldier is selected for duty from the DA FORM 6?

The Soldier with the longest break from performing the assigned duty

What annotation is made on the DA FORM 6 for Soldiers that are not present for duty or available to pull duty?

The letter "A" is entered

What does a "/" (slash) indicate on DA FORM 6?

The date the duty is actually pulled by the Soldier

If a Soldier is unavailable for duty because of arrest or confinement what annotation is made on the DA FORM 6?

The letter "U" is entered
