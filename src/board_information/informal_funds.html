Informal Funds (AR 600-20)

Home

Commanders may authorize informal funds - provide two examples?

Office coffee cup funds, cup and flower, and annual picnic bonds

What expenses may be taken from an informal fund?

Only expenses consistent with the purpose an function of the fund

How many people must be responsible for the custody, accounting and documentation of the fund?

This responsibility must be held by only one person

When should raising money and formal bonds be curtailed?

When such efforts might interfere with CFC or AER campaigns
