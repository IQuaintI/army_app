What is the treatment for carbon monoxide poisoning?

Move the Soldier to open air, keep still and warm, and if necessary administer mouth to mouth and/or CPR, and evacuate to the nearest medical facilty

What are some of the symptoms of carbon monoxide poisoning?

1. Headaches
2. Nausea
3. Dizziness
4. Confusion
5. Ringing in the ears
