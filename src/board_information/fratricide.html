List fratricide prevention methods

Markings, panels, lighting, beacons, and strobes.

What is rule four as it pertains to firearm safety and Fratricide prevention?

Ensure positive identification of the target and its surroundings.

What two processes can leaders implement to help prevent Fratricide and collateral damage?
