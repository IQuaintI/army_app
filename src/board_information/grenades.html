What are the three uses of grenades?

Eliminate threat of enemy soldier in open and entrenched fortified positions, mark positions, conceal operations, surprise the enemy, equalize the threat, destroy or disable enemy equipment when other weapons or munitions are not available, or are in short supply.

Ensure the grenade is upright. Ensure the safety pin and safety clip are in place. There have been incidents in which the grenade was prepackaged to find the safety clip and pin installed. When a left handed person throws a grenade, how should they grasp the grenade?

They will invert the grenade with the pull ring away from the palm of the throwing hand so that the index or middle finger of the free hand can easily remove the pull ring.

When should the safety pin of the grenade be removed?

Only when the grenade is about to be thrown.

When is the only time a soldier should attempt to reinsert the safety pull pin in the grenade?

Never. This could result in wounding or killing a soldier and or damage the equipment.

How is it important to maintain sufficient pressure on the safety lever after removing the safety clip and safety pin?

If pressure on the safety lever is slightly relaxed, it is possible for the striker to rotate and strike the primer while the thrower is still holding the grenade.

When is the only time you should attempt to use a cook off procedure with a fragmentation grenade?

Only use a cook off procedure when the fuse is a setting of 4 seconds or greater in a combat environment. Never attempt this action during training.

What types of grenades should never be cooked off?

MILK, M106, or the MA4, any type of smoke or special purpose grenades. These grenades have a short fuse of 1 to 2.3 seconds and can function in as little as 0.7 seconds.

When cooking off a grenade, how long should you wait before throwing the grenade?

Once you have released the safety lever, count One thousand one , one thousand two, and throw the grenade.

When is it wrong to fire red or green star clusters in the air?

When signaling aircraft, red or green clusters could be mistaken for tracers causing the aircraft to open fire on the friendly ground elements or to withdraw.

What action should be taken when a handheld signal device, power technique, malfunctions?

Keep the signal aimed, pull the cap back and rotate 90 degrees, make two more attempts to fire if it still does not fire, wait 30 seconds, keeping the arm rigid and the signal aimed overhead, return cap to the ejection end of the signal and dispose of it according to the unit SOP.
