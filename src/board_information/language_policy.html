Language Policy (AR 600-20)

Home

What language is considered the operational language of the Army?

English

What are the language requirements for a Soldier in the US Army?

They must maintain sufficient proficiency of the English language to perform their military duties

If a Soldier speaks English as a second language, they must be able to speak to such a proficiency that they are ___?

Clearly understood by everyone who has an official need to know the content of their communication - Conversational english

Can commanders order or require Soldiers speak English in the performance of their military duties?

Yes, the commander can order such action if it is necessary for proper performance of MILITARY functions

Under what conditions can a commander order English be spoken when Soldiers are discussing personal information?

A commander cannot order Soldiers to speak English when the conversation is UNRELATED to military functions
