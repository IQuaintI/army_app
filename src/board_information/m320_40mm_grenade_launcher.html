How do you conduct the functions check on the M320?

1. Clear the host weapon.
2. Clear the grenade launcher.
3. Rotate the selector from SAFE to FIRE and back to SAFE with an audible click.
4. With the selector lever on SAFE, attempt to pull the trigger rearward. The trigger must remain in the forward position.
5. Press the barrel release and allow the barrel to pivot outward.
6. Place the selector lever in the SAFE position.
7. Ensure that the firing pin located on the hammer does not protrude from the bolt face into the chamber.
8. Move the selector lever to the FIRE position.
9. Press the barrel release while attempting to pull the trigger. It must not be possible to pull the trigger enough to raise and release the hammer.
10. With the barrel pivoted outward and the section lever in the FIRE position, pull the trigger, and press the fire finger lightly on the breech face to detect the firing pin protrusion.

Describe the M320/M320A1 grenade launcher.

It is a lightweight grenade launcher that can operate in a standalone or attached configuration. It uses a double-action trigger system. It can accurately engage targets as far away as 350 meters. It has ambidextrous operating controls and a sling mount point allowing the weapon to be fitted to the soldier. The swing out barrel aids the soldier in rapid reloading.

What type of HE rounds can the M320 fire?
