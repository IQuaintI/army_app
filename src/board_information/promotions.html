Promotions (TC 7-21.12, AR 600-8-19)

Home

What is the process for promotions?

Train, select, and promote

WHat does the acronym STEP mean?

Select
Train
Educated
Promote

If a Soldier (PV2-SSG) is fully qualified for promotion but is not recommended for promotion, what action must the first line leader take?

Formally counsel the Soldier when the Soldier obtains full eligibility and every three months thereafter

When counseling a fully eligible, but not recommended Soldier, what must the counseling contain?

The counseling will include information as to why the Soldier was not recommended and what the Soldier must do to correct the deficiencies or qualities that reflect a lack of promotion potential

What are the automatic promotion requirements for promotion to PV2?

6 months time in service

What are the automatic promotion requirements for promotion to PFC?

12 months time in service; 4 months time in grade

What are the automatic promotion requirements for promotion to SPC?

24 months time in service; 6 months time in grade

What can be waived for promotion to PV2?

TIS (Time in service) can be waived from 6 months to 4 months

What can be waived for promotion to PFC?

TIS may be waived from 12 months to 6 months; TIG (Time in grade) may be waived from our months to two months

What can be waived for promotion to SPC?

TIS may be waived from 24 months to 8 months; TIG may be waived from 6 months to 2 months

What does TIS stand for?

Time in Service

What does TIG stand for?

Time in Grade

What are the TIG and TIS requirements in the secondary zone for promotion to SGT?

17 months TIS / 5 months TIG

What are the TIG and TIS requirements in the primary zone for promotion to SGT?

35 months TIS / 6 months TIG

What are the TIG and TIS requirements in the secondary zone for promotion to SSG?

47 months TIS / 6 months TIG

What are the TIG and TIS requirements in the primary zone for promotion to SGT?

71 months TIS / 17 months TIG

For promotion to SGT, what level of SSD must be completed prior to boards appearances?

SSD / DLC 1

For promotion to SSG, what level of SSD must be completed prior to board appearances?

SSD / DLC 2

Must a board promotion at the E5/E6 level include a minority member?:

Yes, if reasonably available

If a Soldier declines promotion, what action must they take?

Submit a memorandum of declination any time after being recommended for promotion

When are hands on tasks authorized when conducting a promotion board?

Hands on tasks are never authorized for promotion boards

What areas will a promotion board normally focus on?

1. Leadership
2. Awareness of military program
3. Knowledge of basic Soldiering skills
4. World affairs

When evaluating a Soldier at a promotion board, board members should consider certain Soldier abilities. What are those abilities?

1. Overall personal appearance
2. Bearing
3. Self-confidence
4. Oral expression
5. Conversational skills
6. Attitude

When does the president of the promotion board vote?

To break a tie

What is the minimum number of voting members a board must have?

3

All voting members must be ____ to the Soldier being recommended for promotion

Senior in rank

With regards to promotion what action will be taken if a Soldier, through their own negligence, failed to take the APFT?

The Soldier will be removed from the recommended list

When is a removal board considered?

Commanders may conduct removal boards when a Soldier's substandard performance or inefficiencies warrant the removal action

Who will the removal board be composed of?

Unbiased members

What action will be taken if a Soldier is promoted in error and became eligible for promotion before the determination of the error was discovered?

The Soldier will retain the promotion

Normally, a Soldier enrolled in the ASAP program cannot be promoted, however a waiver does exist when a certain condition is met. What is that condition?

When the Soldier self refers

If an officer presides over a SGT/SSG board, what is the minimum rank authorized?>

Captain or CW3

Are there any minority requirements for conducting an E5/E6 promotion board.

Yes, voting members of the board WILL include a minority. When this is not possible the promoting authority WILL provide the reason for the absence of a minority in the appointment memorandum

When are promotion board members authorized to leave the board proceedings for the day?

They may only leave when the board proceedings are over and all members must be present for the entire board proceedings

When a removal board is planned, how much notice must the Soldier appearing before the board receive?

At least 15 days

Prior to reducing a Soldiers for inefficiency, what time requirement must be met?

The Soldier must have served in the same unit for at least 90 days prior to being reduced
