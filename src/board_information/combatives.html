To be effective, what must a combatives program be?

Continuous

What are the principle reasons for most combative-related training injuries?

Loss of control

What does DRAW stand for?

Deliberate Risk Assessment Worksheet

What are the five steps in the DRAW (Deliberate Risk Assessment Worksheet)?

1. ID hazards
2. Assess hazards
3. Develop control and make risk decisions
4. Implament controls
5. Supervise and evaluate

What are the four phases of the combatives program?

1. Projectile
2. Striking
3. Clinching
4. Grappling

What are the three types of combative competitions?

1. Open tournaments
2. Command-directed competitions
3. Championships
