ID Cards (AR 600-8-14)

Home

When is the only time an ID card can be photocopied?

To facilitate the Department of Defense's actions. Copying is forbidden otherwise

When can an ID card be confiscated?

1. When the employee is terminated
2. When the ID card is expired, fraudulently used, mutilated, or presented by a person not entitled to use it

Who owns the ID card>

The ID card is government property

What purpose does the CAC serves in the event you are captured?

It serves as your Geneva Convention Card

What are the responsibilities of a CAC holder?

1. Carry the ID card at all times
2. Surrender the card when the military or civilians request it and attempt to retain it in a POW setting
3. Notify the card issuing facility of any changes in status

How long do you have to enroll a newborn in DEERS?

Within 30 days

What benefits are there for family members who were the subject of dependent abuse by the Active Duty sponsor entitled to?

They recieve a DD Form 2755 ID Card are enrolled in DEERS and other benefits
