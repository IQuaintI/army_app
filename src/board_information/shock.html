Name four symptoms of shock

1. Sweaty but cool skin
2. Pale skin, restlessness, confusion, and anxiety
3. Thirst
4. Severe bleeding
5. Confusion
6. Rapid breathing
7. Blotchy blue skin
8. Nausea and/or vomiting

What is the treatment for shock?

1. Move to shelter
2. Shade from direct sunlight
3. Lay casualty on back unless sitting allows for better breathing
4. Loosen clothing
5. Prevent from getting chilled or overheated
