Medical Care (AR 600-20)

Home

If a Soldier refuses to take immunizations what action can be taken against the Soldier?

They can be punished under UCMJ and/or administrative action can be taken against the Soldier for failure to obey a lawful order

What action can be taken if a Soldier refuses medical treatment requiring to save their life, health, or fitness for duty?

If the unit commander is not available, the hospital commander may order the treatment

What action, other than UCMJ, can be taken against a Soldier who refuses to submit to medical care or refuses to submit to dental care and/or dental X-rays?

The Soldier WILL BE referred to a medical board

What action may be taken against a Soldier who refuses immunizations?

Under certain conditions they can be involuntarily administered immunizations
