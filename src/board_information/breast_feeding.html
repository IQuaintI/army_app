Breast Feeding (AR 600-20)

Home

If a soldier wants to breastfeed their child upon return to duty what action must they take?

Notify their chain of command as soon as possible

What type of enviroment must the commander provied for Soldiers that wish to breastfeed?

Provide a private space, other than a restroom, with lactating capabilites, a place to sit, a flat surface (other than the floor), an electrical outlet, and access to a safe water source within a reasonable distance

How long must a commander allow the Soldier to breast feeding breaks?

At least one year after the child's birth

What must commanders due if the Soldier is breast feeding and the unit is conducting field training?

The commander will permit the Soldier the same time and space to express and discard milk with the intent to maintain physiological capability for lactation

What counseling must a commander provide to a Soldier that is breast feeding and may be required to attend field training or mobility training?

They counsel the Soldier and discuss the potential risks/benefits of storing milk during field training and mobility exercises with their medical provider
