Family Care Plan (600-20)

Home

Name three conditions in which a Family Care Plan must be prepared

1. Pregnancy
2. Soldier has minor dependents and is either divorced, widowed, separated, not residing with their spouse, or is married to another service member
3. A family member incapable of self-care
4. A Soldier having sole custody of a child under the age of 18

What action should a commander consider for Soldiers that fail to properly manage personal, martial, or family affairs and fail to maintain family care plans?

Bar to reenlistment

How long does a Soldier have to complete a valid family care plan?

30 days with optional extension by the commander of another 30 days

How often are family care plans recertified?

At least annually

What is the DA FORM 5305?

Family Care Plan

Name three of the six documents that must be attached to the DA FORM 5305 (Family Care Plan)?

1. DA FORM 5841 (Power of Attorney)
2. DA FORM 5840 (Certificate of Acceptance as Guardian or Escort)
3. DD FORM 1172-2 (Application for Identification Cards/DEERS Enrollment)
4. DD FORM 2558 (Authorization to Start, Stop, and/or Change an Allotment)
5. Letter of instruction to the guardian/ escort
6. DA FORM 7666 (Parental Consent)

When can military or civilian authorities obtain care for family members using a Family Care Plan?

When operational or security consideration prevents the Soldier from implementing the plan

If a pregnant Soldier does not have a spouse or is divorced, widowed, seperated, is married to another service member or is not residing with a spouse what action must they take?

They must have a family care plan

If parenthood interferes with military responsibilities what action will the command take?

The Soldier will be counseled on voluntary and involuntary seperation

What regulation covers seperation of Officers and Enlisted personnel?

Officers are covered under AR 600-8-24 and Enlisted are covered under AR 635-200

When should a pregnant Soldier be counseled on Family Care Plan requirements?

As soon as the pregnancy is identified but no later than 90 days prior to the expected date of birth

Who is the sole approving authority of the DA Form 5305?

The unit commander

Who is responsible for conducting Famiy Care Plan counseling?

The unit commander or their designated representative

What is the deployable status of a Soldier without a family care plan?

They are nondeployable
