What is the treatment for poison ivy, oak, or sumac?

1. Gently clean affected area 2 - 3 times a day
2. Wash clothing
3. Apply topical anti-itch cream as needed
4. Cover the affected area
5. Avoid scrathing the affected area
6. Observe for signs of infection
7. Tenderness
8. Warm to touch
9. Seek medical attention
