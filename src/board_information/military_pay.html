What payment options are available to SOldiers with regards to their monthly pay?

They can recieve two payments a month (mid and end of the month) or they can elect to recieve the whole payment on the end of the month

What is Basic Allowance for Subsistence?

Pay provided for subsistence (meals)

What is BAH?

Basic Allowance for Housing which provides funds for housing

Name three types fo BAH pay?

1. BAH I
2. BAH II
3. BAH difference
4. Partial BAH
5. Overseas housing allowance
6. Family Separation Housing

How many discretionary allotments can a Soldier have?

They can have up to six allotments

What is the limit of non-discretionary allotment?

There are no limits on this type of allotment

Name two types of non-discretionary allotments

1. US savings bonds
2. Charitable donations
3. Court ordered child support

What is the emergency partial payment?

For Soldiers assigned in the US, this payment id deemed time sensitive due to unforeseen circumstances and should be paid within 24 hours
