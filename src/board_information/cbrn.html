What are the unmasking procedures using an M256 Kit?

The process takes about 15 minutes. The senior person will select one to two individuals, move to a shady place, have an individual unmask for five minutes and then reseal their masks. Observe for 10 minutes and if no symptoms appear, the leader should consider giving the all clear and continue to monitor for symptoms

What are the unmasking procedures without an M256 Kit?

The process takes about 35 minutes. Find a shady area and use M8/9 paper to check for liquid contamination. Select one or two individuals and have them take a deep breath, hold it, break the seal of their mask, and keep their eyes open. The masks are then cleared and resealed while the individuals are observed for ten minutes. If no symptoms appear, then repeat the cycle of testing and resealing. If no symptoms appear again then the individual(s) unmask for five minutes and then remask under another 10-minute observation. If no symptoms appear then the leader can consider giving the all clear
