What does the acronym COLD stand for?

Clean, (avoid) Overheating, Loose and in layers, and Dry

Soldiers should avoid wearing what types of clothes in cold weather?

Tight and/or dirty clothing

What is wind chill?

It is a measure of the combined effects of wind and temperature

When Soldiers withdraw and do not want to leave the comfort and protection of warm shelter, what is the best remedy?

Soldiers should do physical activty and remain alert and active

What are the keys to staying warm?

Keeping the body clean, dry and warm. Ensuring proper rest and nourishment

What are the four rules for staying warm?

1. Keep in shape
2. Drink plenty of water
3. Eat
4. Maintain a good attitude

Let's say you are changing the tire on a vehicle and the temperature is -10. There are three people in the vehicle. What is one course of action you can take to work through this problem?

Rotate individuals performing the task of changing the tire

Which method of dressing provides the best insulation: One heavy cold weather garment or several layers of clothing?

Several layers of clothing provide more insulation

If you wear boots for a long period of time and your feet become white and wrinkled what action should you take?

Dry the feet and massage them with foot powder. Warm them and put on dry socks

When using a YUKON STOVE with regards to fuel, when is it acceptable to mix fuel types during cold weather operations?

It is NEVER ACCEPTABLE to mix liquid fuels

When is a spark arrestor used on the M1950 Yukon Stove?

Never

Why is dehydration such a significant concern during cold weather operations?

The body loses liquids at a very fast rate in arctic conditions

If water is obtained from glacial stream and is milky, what must be done prior to drinking?

All the water must stand until the sediment settles

In very cold conditions, what must be done to exposed or unprotected skin?

The exposed areas need to be oiled to protect them from the wind and cold

When using stoves in a working area, Soldiers must use caution and be aware of what type of poisoning?

Carbon monoxide poisoning

Eating snow is not a good water substitute because?

It will lower your core temperature

What are the design principles of military cold-weather clothing?

1. Insulate
2. Layer
3. Ventilate

When should your dry damp clothing in your sleeping bag?

Never

How many weeks does it take for a Soldier to adapt to cold weather?

Three to four weeks

What is the purpose of ventilation?

it helps maintain a comfortable body temperature by allowing excess heat and moisture to escape

Why is it important to keep your head covered in a cold enviroment?

Boy heat can be lost through an exposed head and a warm head helps keep the body in equilibrium

Why should you elevate your feet in cold weather when possible?

It aids in body circulation

What is the advantage of using sunglasses during cold wather operations?

Helps prevent snow blindness

Which produces more water when melted - ice or snow?

Ice

What is another name for trench foot?

Immersion foot

How do you dry wet socks and other clothing?

Socks are best dried by placing them against your body. Other pieces of clothing can be dried by hanging them on the outside of your rucksack during movement

How much water should you drink a day to avoid dehydration?

3.5 quarts per day

Where should you place canteens in cold weather operations?

Interior uniform pockets or wrapped in clothing in your rucksack

Define Cacoon Like Existence

Soldiers clothed in several layers of clothing tend to withdraw, have restricted field of vision and hearing. Their spatial awareness, thinking, and reasoning become sluggish

Let's say for some reason you lose or damage a boot. What natural items can you use to fashion makeshift footwear?

Straw or any items that can be woven. The bottoms of the shoes can be stuffed with pine straw or similar material for insulation

Is it better to overdress or to be a little cooler when outside in cold weather?

It is better to be a little cooler than to risk overheating

If you do not have cold weather boots what can you place over your boots to provide additional insulation?

Overshoes or in emergencies even plastic bags. You must pay close attention to your feet to prevent sweating. Keep them dry

What does ECWCS stand for?

Extreme Cold Weather Clothing System

In an emergency what equipment can you use to stay warm if you do not have cold weather clothing?

You can use your training MOPP Suit (CBRN suit) and protective mask. This will assist in helping you stay warm

Since it is impossible to drive tent stakes into frozen ground what method should be used for tent strikes?

The Deadman method

Describe the Deadman tent stake method for cold weather operations?

The tent is wedged horizontally under two blocks of ice, or it is buried in thick ice, or anchored by piling large amounts of broken ice on top of the stake

How far from the tent should stakes or tie down points be located from the tent?

About two meters

How is carbon monoxide vented from the inside of a tent?

By opening the ventilators of the tent near the peak of the tent

When using the Yukon stove, what must be done prior to using solid fuel (wood, coal, etc)?

Remove the burner unit, close the burner opening, and turn over the wire grate so there is space below for draft and ashes

What must be placed down as a base to prevent any stove from melting down into the ice during cold weather operations?

The stove should be placed on a light piece of metal or plywood approximately 25cm x 70cm

What is the average required calorie intake for a Soldier working in cold weather operations?

4,000 calories per day

Why should a Soldier shave in the evening during cold weather operations?

To allow maximum recovery time of the face prior to returning to the cold
