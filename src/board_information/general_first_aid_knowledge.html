If you are under fire and unable to get to get to a casualty to administer treatment, what should you tell the casualty to do?

"Play dead"

Should you attempt to provide first aid when your own life is in danger?

No

When coming upon a casualty what should you do?

Ask in a loud but calm voice:
"Are you okay?"
Afterwards, gently shake the casualty on the shoulder

What is Tactical Combat Casualty Care (TC3)?

What dos the abbreviation AVPU stand for and mean?

It is used to determine the level of consciousness and means:
A = Alert
V = Responds to Voice
P = Responds to pain
U = Unresponsive
