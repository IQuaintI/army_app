Religion (AR 600-20)

Home

Who is the approving authority for accomodation of religious practices that do not require a waiver?

The unit commander

What actions can be taken if a Soldier has worship requirements that conflict with normal availability for duty? For example, worship on days other than Saturday or Sunday, 25-hour Sabbath, or Special Holy Days?

These will be accomodated for except when precluded by military necessity

What action can a commander grant for individuals who desire to observe lengthy holy periods?

They may be grant ordinary leave

What restrictions are placed on religious apparel while in uniform?

They cannot interfere with military duties or interfere with the proper wearing of any authorized article of the uniform

What action can be Soldier request if they have dietary requirements that dictate the eating of specific foods or how meals must be prepared on a religious belief?

They may request an exception to policy to eat seperate rations

How will all requests for accomodations on a religious practice be considered?

On a case-by-case basis

Name three of the five major areas that religious accomodation requests usually fall under?

Worship practices, dietary practices, medical care, wear and appearance of uniform, personal appearance and grooming practices
