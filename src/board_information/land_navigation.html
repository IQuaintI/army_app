What is the best way to obtain accurate ground measurement under cold weather conditions when snow is on the ground and distance measurement is difficult?

1. Two men use a piece of field wire 50 meters long
2. The lead Soldier moves in the desired trailing wire beghind them
3. The second Soldier signals by jerking the wire when the first 50 meters have been used
4. Then the second Soldier moves forward of the first Soldiers positions (leapfrogging) and the process continues until the desired distance is reached
