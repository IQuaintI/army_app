How will an unserviceable flag be destroyed?

It will be destroyed privately and preferably by burning, shredding, or by some other method that does not show irreverence or disrespect to the flag

When is the US flag displayed?

Daily from reveille to retreat

In what position will the flag always be carried?

In the position of honor (The marching right) or if there is a line of flags, it will be positioned in the center

What is the proper method for raising the US-flag to the Half-Staff Position?

It is first hoisted to the top of the pole then lowered to the half-staff position. When the flag is taken down, the flag will be raised to the top of the pole and then lowered for the day

How is the US flag placed on a casket?

Length-wise with the union at the head and over the left shoulder of the deceased

When is a US flag displayed over doorways or arches?

Never

When can the US flag be flown upside down?

In times of distress

How many streamers are attached to the US flag?

190 campaign streamers

What is the size of a garrison flag?

20 ft hoist x 38 ft fly

What is the size of a post flag?

8ft 11 3/8 inches hoist x 17 ft fly

What is the size of a field flag?

6ft 8 inches x 12 ft fly

What is the size of a storm flag?

5 ft hoist x 9ft 6 inches fly

When the US flag is displayed alongside foreign national flags, how is it displayed?

All flags, flagstaff, and flagpoles will be comparable size. Top of all flags should be of equal distance from the ground

When the US flag is displayed either horizontally or vertically against a wall how will it be positioned?

The union is uppermost and to the flag's own right (That is the observer's left when facing the flag)

Who is authorized to place the US flag in mourning?

The President or Secretary of the Army

In order for a unit to be issued a guidon, how many Soldiers must be assigned to the unit?

It must have an authorized strength of at least 20 soldiers

Describe the characteristics of a flag?

Flags are alike on both sides and letters and numerals will read from left to right

WHen may the POW/MIA flag be flown beneath the US flag?

1. On Armed Forces Day (3rd Saturday in May)
2. Memorial Day (Last Monday in May)
3. Flag Day (June 14)
4. Independence Day (July 4)
5. National POW/MIA Recognition Day (3rd Friday in September)
6. Veterans Day (November 11th)
7. Occasions when the installation is sponsoring an event for retirees

Describe the US flag when it is placed in mourning?

A black streamer of black crepe, seven feet long and one foot wide, will be attached to the staff at the center of the streamer immediately below the spearhead of the US flag

When are guidons given to members of the unit as mementos?

Never - They are accountable property and retention by individuals is prohibited

What is a tabard?

A rayon banner of cloth for attachment to the tubing of a herald trumpet

When an automobile plate is in place for a senior official and that official is not occupying the vehicle what action must be taken?

The plate will be covered or removed
