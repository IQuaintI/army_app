What action should be taken if bleeding persists after applying the emergency bandage?

Apply a CAT tourniquet

In first aid what does CAt stand for?

Combat Application Tourniquet

How tight should the CAT be applied?

Two to three inches above the wound

After applying a tourniquet what should be written on the casualty with a marker?

The letter "T" should be placed on the casualty along with the time of application - typically on the forehead
