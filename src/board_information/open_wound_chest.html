Prior to applying the bandage to an open chest wound, what should you have the casualty do?

Fully exhale

After treating an open chest wound, how should you position the casualty?

Place them in the sitting position or injured side down

When treating an open chest wound, what type of material is placed between the wound and the dressing?

Apply airtight material over the wound and the casualty's outer wrapping for the field dressing

When treating an open chest wound how far should the material extend beyond the edge of the wound?

At least two inches
