Destruction of Weapons (TC 3-21.75, TC 3-23.30)

Home

When will a military weapon of any type be destroyed?

This is a command decision and should only be taken as a last resort to prevent the enemy from capturing or using the weapon.

What conditions should weapons or equipment be destroyed under?

1. To prevent opposition from using weapons or equipment against you
2. To prevent opposition from acquiring sensitive information
3. If there is equipment and facilities available to safely do so

When destroying grenades in the open, what is the danger area to all personnel should be clear of?

600 meters

Once the grenades have been properly destroyed by approved methods, how long should leaders wait before confirming or inspecting the burn donation pit to ensure all grenades were properly destroyed and the enemy cannot use them at all?

No attempt should be made to validate destruction. Attempting to validate the destruction could inadvertently cause injury or death.

If destruction of grenades is ordered, what factor should be considered before destroying the grenades?

1. Selection of a site
2. Observe appropriate ammunition safety precautions.
3. Protect assets not being demolished from fragmentation
4. Notify adjacent military units.
