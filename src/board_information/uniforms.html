Describe the short female hairstyle.

Short hair is defined as hair length that exceeds no more than one inch from the scalp (excluding bangs). There is no minimum hair length for female soldiers. Bangs may not fall below the eyebrow, may not interfere with the wear of all headgear, must lie neatly against the head and not be visible underneath the front of the headgear. The width of the bangs may extend to the hairlines at the temple.

Describe the female medium hair style.

Medium hair is defined as hair length that does not exceed beyond the lower edge of the collar in all uniforms, and extends more than 1 inch from the scalp. Medium hair may fall naturally in uniform and is not required to be secured. When worn loose, graduated hair styles are acceptable, but the length must not exceed 1 inch difference in length from the front to the back. Layered hairstyles are also authorized so long as each hair is length as measured from the scalp to the hair's end is generally the same length, giving a tapered appearance. No portion of the bulk of the hair as measured from the scalp will exceed 2 inches. Females are authorized medium length ponytails on . In all uniforms the unsecured hair will be worn, centered in the back of the head. Placement of ponytail will not be on the side or top of the head, being no wider than the width of the head, will not extend beyond the lower edge of the collar and will not interfere with proper wear of authorized army head gear.

Describe the female long hair style.

Long hair is defined as hair length that extends beyond the lower edge of the collar. Long hair will be neatly and inconspicuously fastened or pinned above the lower edge of the collar (except when worn in accordance with para 3-2). No portion of the bulk of the hair as measured from the scalp as styled will exceed 2 inches except the bun which is worn on the back of the head and will not exceed 3 1/2 inches from the scalp. Female soldiers are authorized to wear long ponytails in utility uniforms when conducting physical training or tactical operations. The long ponytail is authorized walking to and from the designated areas of physical training. Additionally, while female soldiers are wearing equipment such as but not limited to combat vehicle crewmen (CVC) helmets or advanced combat helmets (ACH), they will be authorized to wear their hair and a ponytail or a long braid secured in their utility uniform top.

Describe the male hairstyle.

The hair on top of the head must be neatly groomed. The length and bulk of the hair may not be excessive and must present a neat and conservative appearance. The hair must present a tapered appearance. A tapered appearance is one where the outline of the soldier's hair conforms to the shape of the head, curving inward to the natural termination point at the base of the neck. When the hair is combed, it will not fall over the ears or eyebrows or touch the collar except for the closely cut hair on the back of the neck. The block cut fullness in the back is permitted to a moderate degree as long as the tapered look is maintained. Males are not authorized to wear braids, cornrows, twists, dreadlocks, or locks in uniform or in civilian clothes while on duty. Hair cuts with a single un-tapered patch of hair on top of the head, not consistent with natural hair loss, are considered eccentric and are not authorized. Examples include when the head is shaved around the strip of hair down the center of the head, around a U-shaped hair area, or on a patch of hair on front of the top of the head. Hair that is completely shaved or trimmed closely to the scalp is authorized.

Describe multiple hairstyles

Medium and long hair may be styled in braids, cornrows, twists, or locks. Each braid, cornrow, twist, or lock will be of uniform dimension, have a diameter no greater than 1.5 inches and present a neat professional and welcoming appearance. The spacing between them must be uniform. Each hairstyle may be worn against the scalp or loose. When worn loose, such hairstyles must be worn per medium hair length guidelines. Ends must be secured inconspicuously.

What are the additional hairstyle guidelines per AR670-1?

Faddish and exaggerated styles include shaved portions of the scalp other than the neckline designs cut in the hair, unsecured ponytails, except during physical training, and unbalanced or lopsided hairstyles are prohibited. Hair will be styled so as to not interfere with the proper wear of all uniform headgear. All headgear will fit snugly and comfortably around the largest part of the head, without bulging or distortion from the intended shape of the headgear and without excessive gaps. When headgear is worn, hair should not protrude at distinct angles from under the edges.

Describe how females will apply cosmetics.

Females are authorized to wear cosmetics while in uniform provided they are applied modestly and conservatively and that they complement both the soldier's complexion and the uniform. Leaders at all levels must exercise good judgment when interpreting and enforcing this policy. Commander may direct a removal of authorized jewelry when prohibited for safety or health reasons.

When a soldier is not in compliance with the tattoo or branding policy, what action must the commander take?

Counsel the soldier in writing on a DA form 4856.

Can a soldier cut a part of their hair if no natural part exists.

Soldiers who have a texture of hair that does not part naturally may cut a part into the hair or style the hair with one part. The part will be one straight line, not slanted or curved, and will fall on the area where the soldier would normally part their hair. Soldiers cannot cut shapes or cut designs into their hair or scalp.

Describe proper wear of hair devices and give them examples of authorized hair devices.

Hair holding devices are authorized only for the purpose of securing the hair. Soldiers will not use hair holding devices for decorative purposes. All hair holding devices must be plain and of color as close to the soldier's hair as it is possible or clear. Authorized devices include but not limited to small plain scrunchies, barrettes, combs, pins, clips, rubber bands, and hairs or headbands. Such devices should conform to the natural shape of the head.

What requirements will female soldiers comply when wearing lipstick?

Soldiers will not wear shades of lipstick that distinctly contrast with the natural color of the lips, that the track from the uniform or fat is eccentric or exaggerated. Females or soldiers are authorized to wear solid color shades of lipstick that are not extreme. Extreme colors include but are not limited to purple, bright pink, bright red, gold, blue, black, hot pink, green, yellow, ombre, and fluorescent neon colors. Natural colors to include tinted glasses are authorized. The optional wear of lip liner is authorized but colors must match the shade of lipstick being worn.

Describe the proper wear of a mustache.

When mustaches are permitted, they must be maintained to a length not to exceed two inches when measured from the bottom of the chin. Beard hair longer than two inches must be rolled and or tied to achieve the required length. Beards must be worn in a neat and conservative manner that presents a professional appearance. Soldiers may use styling products to groom or hold the beard in place, but may not use petroleum-based products if wearing a protective mask or in training. The bulk of a soldier's beard may not impair the ability to operate the assigned weapon, military equipment, or machinery.

Describe the wear requirements for bracelets.

Bracelets are limited to medical alert bracelets missing in action, prisoner of war killed in action, and religious bracelets similar in size and appearance to identification bracelets. Describe earrings authorized for wear by female soldiers. Earrings may be screw on, clip on, or post type earrings in gold, silver, white, pearl, or diamond. The earring must not exceed six millimeters or a quarter inch in diameter and they must be unadorned and spherical. When worn, the earrings must fit snugly against the ear. Females may wear earrings only as a matched pair and only one earring per earlobe.
