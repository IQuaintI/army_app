Name the Army values

1. Loyalty
2. Duty
3. Respect
4. Selfless-service
5. Honor
6. Integrity
7. Personal courage

Define loyalty

Bear true faith and allegiance to the US Constitution, the Army , your unit, and fellow Soldier

Define duty

Fulfill your obligations

Define respect

Treat people as they should be treated

Define selfless-service

Put the welfare of the Nation, the Army, and your subordinates before your own

Define honor

Live up to Army values

Define integrity

Do what is right - legally and morally

Define personal courage

Face fear, danger, or adversity - physical or moral
