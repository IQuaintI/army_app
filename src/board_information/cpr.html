What is the ratio of breaths to chest compressions?

30 compressions per 2 breaths

When giving chest compressions how many should be given at one time?

30

When giving chest compressions how deep should te compressions go?

At least two inches with each compression

How should chest compressions be delivered?

Straight down on the casualty's breastbone

How fast should chest compressions be done?

30 compressions at the rate of 100 in a smooth fashion

When should you stop chest compressions?

1. When the casualty is revived
2. You are too tired to continue
3. You are relieved by a health care professional
4. The casualty is pronounced dead
5. A second rescuer states they know CPR and aids you in performing two-person CPR

If you cannot use mouth to mouth, what method of rescue breathing should you use?

Mouth to nose
