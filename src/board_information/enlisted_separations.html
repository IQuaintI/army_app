Why should every means to rehabilitate a Soldier be taken before a separation action is initiated?

The Army makes a substantial investment into every Soldier therefore it is considered a waste to relieve one without effort

If a Soldier is identified for potential separation, what actions should normally be taken prior to initiating separations?

Attempt to correct substandard performance or behavior using corrective training, counseling, retraining, or rehabilitation

Why is it important to seperate Soldiers who are undisciplined and/or display substandard performance?

Separation avoids the high cost associated with continued pay, administrative efforts, degradation of morale, and substandard mission performance

What are the requirements to separate a Soldier?

The Soldier must be formally notified of their deficiencies. At least one formal counseling session must have taken place prior to separation proceedings. There must also be evidence that the deficiencies continued after the formal counseling

Can a Soldier be processed for separation and the separation be suspended (similar to suspended punishment under an Article 15)?

A highly deserving Soldier may be given a probation period to show successful rehabilitation before the Soldier's enlistment or obligated services expires. The probation period cannot exeed 12 months
