Line of Investigations (AR 600-8-4)

When conducting a line of duty investigation on a Soldier who has caused intentional harm to themselves, is this considered a line of duty situation?

No, if a Soldier causes injury to themselves intentionally it cannot be a line of duty "YES". The AR states that if a person who becomes a casualty as a result to their own intentional misconduct or wilful negligence, it is NEVER considered a line of duty situation.

What happens to a Soldier if a line of duty investigation determines the Soldier is at fault for the injury?

The Soldier stands to lose substantial benefits as a consequence of their actions

When is a line of duty investigation considered a "YES" without an investigation?

In cases of disease or injury as a result of enemy action or attack by terrorist, or due to natural disaster while passenger in a contracted commercial carrier or military vessel

Can a line of duty investigation result in disciplinary and other administrative actions?

Yes, it can result in administrative action if deemed necessay through the investigation

What does LD stand for?

Line of Duty investigation

When in an AWOl status, what is the LD determination due to injury or illness?

Not in the line of duty

How are pregnancies treated with regard to LD investigations?

They are exempt from LD investigations

How are abortions performed outside the local laws handled with regard to LD investigation?

They are subject to LD investigations when complications or after effects hinder duty performance

What is a finding of a LD investigation for a Soldier determined by medical authority to be totally and physically incapacitated for more than 24 consecutive hours soley due to alcohol or drug abuse?

The LD find will be "Not in the line of duty"

What is the LD finding for a Soldier who commits suicide, or who attempts a suicide attempt?

The LD would be considered "In the Line of Duty" as long as mental, emotional, and/or physical disorders did not exist prior to the service

How long does a Soldier have to appeal a LD finding?

The appeal must occur within 30 days after receipt of the LD determination

What is the finding of a LD investigation for a Soldier that has infkicted self-harm on purpose but is otherwise considered mentally sound?

This would not be considered within the line of duty

If a LD is found to be favorable for the Soldier, can the Soldier still be subject to administrative or disciplinary action?

Yes, action can still be taken if a LD is found "YES"

Is a line of duty investigation a punitive action?

No, it is an administrative action

Does a report being late have influence over the final decision of the LD?

No, this fact cannot be used as a basis for approval or disapproval

How are accident and safety investigations and reports conducted under AR 385-40 used in determining the LD status of a Soldier?

These reports may not be used as evidence or to obtain evidence in determining the LD status of a Soldier

What action must be taken if a LD is not completed in the appropriate time limits?

If not completed within the given time, the reason for lateness should be made part of the remarks section of the DA Form 2173 for informal reports and the DD Form 261 for formal reports

What would the LD finding be for a Soldier driving a military vehicle who decides to deviate from their assigned mission to het lunch at a facility?

An unjustified deviation would be considered as AWOL and for LD purposes it would be "Not in the line of duty"

With regards to a LD, what would be the determination be for a Soldier who contracts a venereal disease?

This action alone does not support a "Not in the line of duty" finding if the Soldier complied with regulations and reported and recieved proper treatment

Define simple negligence and its impact on a LD investigation

Mere violation of military regulations, orders, or instructions, or of civil or criminal law is usually considered no more than simple negligence and is not considered misconduct
