What lubricant should be used on your weapon during cold weather operations?

CLP

When a cold weapon is taken into a heated area, what happens to the weapon?

It sweats.

After a cold weapon has been in a warm area for at least 1 hour, what action should be taken?

The weapon should be cleaned to remove any condensation, otherwise the condensation will freeze and the weapon is returned to the cold area and the weapon will malfunction.

How long will a cold weapon continue to sweat when brought into a warm area?

About 1 hour.

Using CLP on a weapon in cold weather operations helps prevent this weapon from sweating.
