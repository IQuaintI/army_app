What subject does TC 3-21.75?

Warrior Ethos and Soldier Combat Skills

What does the acronym ES2 stand for?

Every Soldier a Sensor

What is the recommended interval between Soldiers when on patrol during night ops?

One to three meters

Whenever you approach or observe a danger area, what should your first course of action be, if at all possible?

Bypass the danger area

What is a battle drill?

A collective action executed by a platoon or smaller element, without application of a deliberate decision-making process. It is vital to the success in combat or critical to preserve life. Initiated on cue, such as an enemy action or your leader's orders and is trained to reponse to that stimulus. Requires minimal leaders orders and is standard through the Army

Name the Warrior Drills

1. React to contact (visual, IED, direct fire)
2. React to ambush (Near)
3. React to ambush (Far)
4. React to indirect fire
5. React to direct chemical attack
6. Break contact
7. Dismount a vehicle
8. Evacuate wounded personnel from vehicle
9. Establish security at the halt

With regard to urban warfare, what is a "Loophole"?

A hole created in a wall as an alternative fire position so that a Soldier does not have to fire from a window

What are the requirements when using a loophole firing position?

The Solder must stay well back from the loophole, so the muzzle of teh weapon does not protrude through the wall concealing the muzzle flash

What is the purpose of the finger measurement method?

To determine the lateral distance from a known reference point to a target

When you are required to enter a building during urban combat and there is no other option available but to enter through a doorway or window. What can be used to check for trip wires?

Silly string is a good choice for this situation as it shoots out a good distance and will rest lightly on any trip wires
