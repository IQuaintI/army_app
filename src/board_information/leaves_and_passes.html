Leaves and Passes (AR 600-8-10)

What action will be taken if a Soldier has a leave balance of 60 days and waits until late in the year to take leave?

The Soldier will be informed that they risk loss of leave if the operational situation requires their presence

How many days of leave are accrued each month?

2.5 days per month

What are the rules for receiving payment of accrued leave (selling leave)?

The Soldier may sell 60 days of leave one time during a military career

What are the rules for Soldiers to recieve Special Leave Accrual?

Soldiers who serve in an area in which they are entitled to hostile fire or immenent danger pay for at least 120 days are authorized special leave accrual

What does special leave accrual permit?

Soldiers are allowed to carry foward up to 120 days of leave at the end of a fiscal year

Is special leave accrual automatic?

it must be requested by a Soldier

What is advanced leave?

Leave granted in anticipation of accrual or leave during the remaining period of active duty (Going negative on leave)

What are the two types of enviromental and morale leave?

Funded and unfunded

What is rest and recuperation leave?

Chargeable leave authorizing use of ordinary leave to allow Soldiers leave away from hostile fire and imminent danger areas or non-chargeable leave of up to 15 days used in conjunction with benefits under the R&R leave program

How much leave can a Soldier recieve after child birth?

30 days or less

What is covalescent leave?

Non-chargeable absences from duty granted to expedite a Soldier's return to full duty after illness, injury, or childbirth

A regular pass will not exceed how many days?

It will not exceed three days in length to include public holiday weekends or public holiday periods specifically granted by the President

What are the two types of Special Passes?

Three and four days

How much emergency leave can be granted for immediate family or loco parentis?

Up to 30 days

What must a Soldier do before taking leave outside the United States?

They must obtain approval from the chain of command

What is enviromental leave

Morale leave that is a chargeable leave program at overaseas locations to make use of space available travel or commercial transportation

What does EML stand for?

Enviromental Morale Leave

What conditions must exist exist to establish an EML program?

1. Extra-ordinary difficult living conditions
2. Geographic isolation
3. Inadequate commercial transportation
4. Lack of adequate recreational facilities and cultural opportunities
5. Location must be truly isolated, austere, or enviromentally depressed to be designated for EML

From what location must a Soldier begin and end a regular pass

It begins or ends on post , at a duty location, or at the location from where the Soldier normally commutes to duty. The Soldier must be physically at one of these locations when departing or returning from a regular pass

What is the length of a three day regular pass?

A three-day pass which includes a national holiday, begins at the end of the normal duty day on a Friday afternoon and terminates at the beginning of the normal duty day on the 4th day (Tuesday)

What is the rule when granting back to back passes?

Passes may not be granted back to back without duty day in between the absences

What must a special pass include with regards to days of the week?

It must include at least two days

Is Red Cross verification required for emergency leave?

No, emergency leave may be granted with or without Red Cross verification. Most Soldiers are mature and responsible so their emergency needs can be considered on their merits

How may a Soldier request a leave extension?

In person, by telephone, by message, or by other convenient means through appropriate overseas command, or through the Red Cross, or throught the nearest military base near the leave address

What is the normal time limit required to submit a leave extension request?

Request will be submitted 72 hours in advance of expiration of leave

What is the maximum time that is allowed for authorized proceed time?

Four days

When dropping off or picking up a privately owned vehicle from a port, is the leave chargeable or non-chargeable?

Non-chargeable

How much time is granted for delivering and picking up a privately owned vehicle from a port?

It varies but it is limited to the time necessary to reach port, accomplish the necessary delivery or pick up requirements and return to the duty station
