What are the most important responsibilities for an NCO?

Mission accomplishment and the welfare of their Soldiers

Every Soldier has two types of responsibility, what are they?

Requirement to perform your duties to the best of your ability, personal conduct, & appearance

Define rank responsibility

Those responsibilities based on rank, duty position, and even geographical location

Define accountability

The requirement to answer to superiors for mission accomplishment. The obligation to answer for properly using delegated authority. Leaders are accountable for what they do

Define moral responsibility

The responsibility to treat others with dignity and respect. You will not tolerate sexual, racial, or other types of discrimination or harassment

In addition to accomplishing the mission and looking out for the welfare of their Soldiers, NCO's have three additional responsibilities, what are they?

1. Maintain discipline
2. Maintain property accountability
3. Train Soldiers
