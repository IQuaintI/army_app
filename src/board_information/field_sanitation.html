Describe a cross tree type latrine

Find two trees close together. Horizontally tie a rope, wire, or branch about 18 inches off the ground between the two trees. Then tie a second rope, branch, or wire about 36 inches above the first horizontal branch. Dig a wide hole beneath the first branch, wire, or rope. A Soldier will sit on the first branch while resting their back on the second branch and use the latrine
