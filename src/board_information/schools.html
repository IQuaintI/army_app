What is the goal of NCO training and the NCOES?

To prepare NCOs to lead and train Soldiers who work and fight under their supervision, and assist their leaders in executing the missions

What action is taken if a Soldier receives a profile when enrolled in PME?

They will be evaluated by the Commandant to determine if they are capable of meeting graduation requirement

If a Soldier has a temporary profile that prevents full participation in a PME course, what action must command take?

If the temporary profile is not a result of operational deployment the Soldier will be removed or deferred from school attendance until the temporary profile is removed

If a Soldier fails their initial APFT test upon enrolling in a school, how long do they have to retake the APFT?

No earlier than seven days and no later than 24 days

What does the BLC training course focus on?

Basic immaterial leadership training

What does the ALC training course focus on?

Leadership training along with branch-specific and company level training

What does the SLC training course focus on?

Advanced, branch-specific, platoon and company-level training

What level of Self-Development training is mandatory before attending BLC?

SSD1

What level of Self-Development training is mandatory before attending ALC?

SSD2

What level of Self-Development training is mandatory before attending SLC?

SSD3

If a Soldier receives a 1059 that states "failed to achieve core standards", when are they eligible to attend another PME course?

They are eligible to attend after six months from the date of dismissal

What directive re-designed the Army Profile System?

Army Directive 2016-97

What commanders are considered principle training managers?

Battalion commanders

Define constructive course credit

Constructive credit is granted in lieu of course attendance based on previous leadership experience and/or past academic training experiences

Define operational course credit

Operational credit may be granted to individuals in lieu of course attendance based on operational experience

What document does a Soldier receive upo graduating of a PME course?

DA FORM 1059

What does NCOPDS stand for?

Non-commissioned Officer Professional Development System

What is the intent of SSD?

Structured Self-Development

What is the intent of SSD?

To bridge the operational and institutional domains and set the conditions for continuous growth.

What action will be taken if a Soldier fails the APFT while enrolled in the PME?

The Soldier will be removed from the course

What action is taken if the Soldier does not meet the height and weight standards at the PME?

The Soldier is authorized one re-screening and it is administered no earlier than 7 days and no later than 24 days after the initial failure

What action will be taken if a Soldier fails the re-screening?

The Soldier will be removed from the course

What action must be taken by the chain of command take prior to dismissing a Soldier from a PME course?

The Soldier is to be counseled by the chain of command

How long does a Soldier have to respond to the receipt of written notification of dismissal from PME?

The Soldier must respond within two duty days

How long does a Soldier have to appeal a dismissal action from PME?

The Soldier has the right to appeal within seven duty days to the school Commandant or Commander

What action is taken when a Soldier appeals a dismissal action from PME?

The Soldier will remain actively enrolled in the course pending of their appeal

Name the four types of deferments for PME

Compassionate, medical, operational, and non-commissioned officer evaluation system deferment process

How are declinations from PME considered for NCOs?

Declination from NCOES courses will not be considered favorably for active-duty Soldiers

How does a flag impact a Soldier attending military institutional training?

They will not be selected, scheduled, or attend institutional training

Explain equivalent credit

Credit for a PME course may be granted to individuals in lieu of course attendance based on courses possessing comparable terminal learning objectives
