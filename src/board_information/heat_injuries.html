Name the types of heat injuries and heat related conditions?

1. Heat cramps
2. Heat exhaustion
3. Heat stroke
4. Heat rash
5. Sunburn

How much time does proper heat acclimatization require?

Three to five days but full acclimatization can take up to two weeks

What is heat exhaustion?

It is dehydration and loss of body salt

Why are heat exhaustion and heat cramps considered 'canaries in the coal mine'?

Because if these things are not treated properly they can lead to a heat stroke

What are the symptoms of heat exhaustion?

1. Dizziness, weakness, and/or fainting
2. Skin is cool and moist
3. Nausea and/or headaches

What is the treatment for heat exhaustion?

1. Move to a cool shaded area
2. Loosen clothing
3. Pour water on casualty and fan to increase cooling
4. Provide at least one quart of water to replace lost fluids
5. Elevate legs and feet above the heart
6. Seek medical attention

What are the symptoms of heat stroke (sunstroke)?

1. No sweating
2. Hot dry skin
3. Headaches
4. Dizziness
5. Nausea
6. Vomiting
7. Rapid pulse
9. Seizures
10. Confusion
11. Unconsciousness

What is the treatment for heat stroke (sunstroke)?

1. Move to a cool shaded area
2. Loosen clothing
3. Remove outer clothing
4. Immerse in cool water or if bath is not available, spray with water on head and body
5. Fan to increase cooling
6. If conscious, give at least one quart of water to replace lost fluids

What is the treatment for heat cramps?

1. Move to shade
2. Loosen clothing
3. Give at least one quart of water to replace lost fluids
4. Monitor
5. If cramps persist, seek medical attention

What are the signs of heat cramps?

1. Muscle cramps in the arms, legs, and/or stomach
2. Pale skin
3. Dizziness and extreme thirst
