What is a closed fracture?

One in which the bone does not break the skin

What is an open fracture?

One in which the bone pierces the skin

What are the signs and symptoms associated with a fracture?

1. Deformity
2. Tenderness
3. Swelling
4. Pain
5. Inability to move the affected area
6. Protruding bone
7. Bleeding
8. Discolored skin at the site of the fracture

What are open fractures subject to?

Contamination and infection
