EPW / Internees / Detainees (AR 190-8, STP 21-1)

Home

You are about to search a detainee, what must you avoid?

Crossing the line of sight or firing position of the person performing overwatch

During detainee operations how should the person performing overwatch position themselves?

At a 45-degree angle and out of reach from the detainee

What are the five S's for detainee operations?

1. Search
2. Segregate
3. Silence
4. Separate
5. Safeguard

What is the policy for photographing, filing, and videotaping, EPW's

It is strictly prohibited unless done by Internal Internment Facility Administration or for Intelligence/Counterintelligence purposes

If an EPW is treated inhumanly as a result of combat stress, what action it taken?

The acts will be reported through the chain of command. These acts are punishable under UCMj and international law. The stress of combat is not justifiable reason for inhuman treatment

What latitude will be afforded EPWs with regards to their religion?

They will enjoy latitude in exercise of their religious practices, including attendance at the service of their faith, on condition that they comply with the disciplinary routine prescribed by military authorities. Adequate space will be provided where religious services are held

Name three acts the EPWs are protected against

1. Acts of violence including rape
2. Froced prostitution
3. Assault
4. Theft
5. Insults
6. Public curiosity
7. Bodily injury and reprisals of any kind including murder
8. Torture
9. Corporal punishment
10. Mutilation
11. Taking of hostages
12. Sensory deprivation
13. Execution

When is currency confiscated from en EPW?

Only on the orders of a commissioned officer and the EPW will be given a receipt

What personal effecs are EPWs allowed to retain?

Jewelry, helmets, canteens, protective masks and chemical protective garments, clothing, identification cards and tags, badges of rank and nationality, Red Cross brassards, articles having personal/sentimental/religious value, and items used for eating except knives and forks

Why is it important to move a POW to the rear as soon as possible?

They are of intelligence value and need to be interrogated as soon as possible

Why is it important to silence POWs?

It helps prevent them from discussing what information they will provide, prevents planning potential escapes, riots, etc.

What does safeguarding mean?

Protecting POWs from harm until they are released from your custody

What are you looking for when searching a POW?

Information that can be of intelligence value, weapons, and to verify they will not present a threat to you or others

When is it acceptable to for EPWs to be subject to medical or scientific experiments?

Never

How tight should disposable restraints be when applied to a detainee?

Tight enough to secure the detainee but loose enough to allow one finger between the restraint and the detainee's wrist
