Unit Memorial Services (AR 600-20)

Home

What is the purpose of unit memorial ceremonies and services?

To show respect for the service of a fallen Soldier and to offer support to the unit

Unit memorial ceremonies will be conducted for which member of the unit?

Any members of the unit that dies while assigned to the unit regardless of death to include suicides

When can a commander request an exception to policy NOT to conduct a ceremony?

When the deceased Soldier has been convicted of a capital offense under Federal or State law in which the person was sentenced to life imprisonment without parole or death or convicted of a crime that would bring discredit upon the Army

Who must approve the exception not to conduct a ceremony?

The first GO in the chain of command
