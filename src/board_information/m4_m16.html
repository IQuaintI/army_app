Describe how to perform a function check on the M4.

Remove the weapon. Place it on safe. Pull the charging handle to the rear. Release it. Pull the trigger. The hammer shouldn't fall. Place the switch on semi.
Pull trigger. Hammer should fall. Hold trigger to rear and charge weapon. Release trigger with a slow, smooth motion so the trigger is fully forward. Listen for audible click.
Pull trigger. Hammer should fall. Place the switch on burst. Charge weapon one time. Hammer should fall. Hold trigger to the rear. Charge weapon three times. Release trigger. Squeeze trigger. Hammer should fall.

What are the proper steps to mechanical zero the M4 series rifle carbine?

1. Set rear apertures by positioning the aperture so the unmarked aperture is up and the 0-200 meter aperture is down.
2. Set windage by turning the windage knob to align the index mark on the 0-200 meters aperture with the long center index line on the rear sight assembly.
3. Turn the elevation knob counter-clockwise until the rear sight assembly rests flush with the detachable carrying handle and the 6/3 marking is aligned with the index line on the left side of the carrying handle.
