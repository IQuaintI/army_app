What are the first aid procedures for a head injury?

1. Maintain patient's airway using the jaw thrust maneuver
2. Dress head wounds
3. Control bleeding
4. Treat for shock
5. Seek medical aid
6. Monitor for convulsions or seizures

What are the signs of increaed head pressure?

1. Headaches, nausea, vomiting
2. Possible unconsciousness
3. Change in pupil size
4. Lateral loss of motor nerve function
5. One side of the body becomes paralyzed
6. Change in respitory rate
7. Elavated body temperature
8. Restlessness

When treating a head wound and brain tissue is present, it is important that you do not?

Apply pressure to or try to replace the brain tissue

What are the symptoms of a concussion?

1. Unconsciousness followed by confusion
2. Temporary loss of memory
3. Headache or seeing double
4. May or may not get skull fractures

What is a contusion?

An internal bruise or injury that is more serious than a concussion
