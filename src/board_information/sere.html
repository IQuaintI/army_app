Name guidelines you should follow in regards to evasion?

1. Have a plan. Be positive, use established procedures, follow your evasion plan, be patient, drink water, conserve strength, rest, and stay out of sight
2. Avoid odors as they give you away (Examples: scented soaps, insect repellant, tobacco, shaving cream)
3. Find a suitable place for recovery, a place to rest, and remember where friendly territory is
4. Use camouflage, chose an area that is least likely to be searched, know your escape route, and remember that a moving target is easy to spot so only move when you have to
5. If captured by the enemy, make every effort to resist and cause confusion among the enemy
6. Make every effort to escape and it is best to escape when you know where you and where allied space is

How are the ACU pants used as floatation devices?

The legs are tied together and the buttons are buttoned. The pants are slung over the head to trap air into the pants. Place your head in the opening between the legs, trapping the water inside. The pants will then float. Continue to splash air into the pants to maintain the air in the pants.
