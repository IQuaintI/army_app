Typically, how is hyponatremia induced in Soldiers?

By consuming too much water at one time

What are the signs and symptoms of hyponatremia?

1. Mental status change
2. Vomiting
3. History of consumption of large amounts of water
4. Poor food intake
5. Abdomen distended
6. Large amounts of clear urine

What are the first aid procedures for hyponatremia?

1. Do not give water or fluids by IV
2. If awake, allow Soldier to consume salty foods or snacks
3. Seek medical aid
4.Evacaute Soldier to medical facility
